About Me

I grew up fascinated by computers - playing on old Pentium machines and watching how interfaces came to life.Even then, I was drawn to design, but I carried a deep fear that coding wasn't meant for someone like me. I doubted my abilities and underestimated myself. Creativity, however, was always my part of my life. Whether I was baking, crafting, or creating by hand, making things gave me a sense of calm and purpose. Later, while working with marketing and automation, something shifted. I realized that to grow fully, I had to face that fear and learn to code. The journey was challenging and often uncomfortable, but I stayed committed and completed a two-year program at Noroff. Today, I bring together creativity, empathy, and technical skills to build thoughtful, user-centered digital experiences and I’m excited to grow my professional career in an environment where learning, quality, and collaboration are valued.
